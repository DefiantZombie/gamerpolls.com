We are currently in a closed beta testing period. This is an invite-only beta, so please don't contact us to get access. Currently the only benefit of being a beta tester is the ability to create polls. All users are allowed to vote in polls if they have the link, and only if the poll creator didn't set any limits.

GamerPolls

Welcome to GamerPolls, a place to get the cold-hard opinion from gamers. We are geared towards Twitch streamers creating polls, with options to make it a subscriber/follower-only polls, or a unique "versus" mode where supporters and non-supported clash their votes against eachother.
